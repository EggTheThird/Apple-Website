STEVE WOZNIAK

Steve Jobs enlisted the creativity and technical skill of Steve Wozniak while Jobs was still working for Atari.When the two made Breakout for Atari, Jobs deceived Woz into taking on 350$ of the 5000$ pay.Following this deception, Jobs and Wozniak worked together to create Apple.Wozniak would go on to quit working for the Apple Company in 1984 and sell a majority of his stock in the company.


LISA AND CHRISSAN BRENNAN

In 1978, Jobs and Chrissan Brennan had a daughter out of wedlock and named her Lisa. Shortly after, Jobs named the design of his personal computer the Apple Lisa, most likely in her honor. Jobs, however, denied his paternal status of Lisa Brennan-Jobs for the entirety of her childhood, even going to court and denying the legitimacy of a DNA paternity test. Despite his wealth, Steve refused to pay child support, leaving Chrissan Brennan to raise their daughter in poverty.


JOHN SCULLY

Jobs was fired by the board of Apple in 1985, after heated disagreement with the CEO John Scully. Jobs was taken off the engineering team for the Macintosh because he was considered too demanding of his employees and accused of spending too much money and company resources on the Macintosh.This accusation was based in the computer's million dollar advertisement in 1984.
